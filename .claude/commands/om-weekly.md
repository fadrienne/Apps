# Weekly Synthesis

Review vault activity for the past 7 days. Bridge between daily standups and quarterly reviews.

## 1. What Happened

Run `git log --oneline --since="7 days ago"`. List all notes modified. Group by area: work, org, perf, brain.

## 2. North Star Alignment

Read `brain/North Star.md`. For each current goal:
- Is there evidence of progress this week?
- Any goals receiving zero attention? Flag as drift.
- Any goals completed? Suggest archiving.

## 3. Cross-Session Patterns

What topics appeared in 3+ sessions this week? These are signals — either recurring blockers, active focus areas, or emerging decisions worth capturing in `brain/Patterns.md`.

## 4. Uncaptured Wins

Invoke `brag-spotter` to find accomplishments from the week not yet in `perf/brag/`.

## 5. Competency Coverage

Which competencies were exercised this week? Are they linked in the relevant work notes?

## 6. Output

Produce: weekly summary, alignment status, pattern list, uncaptured wins, competency coverage table, 3 forward actions.

**This output is transient by default.** Offer to promote findings to `perf/brag/` or `brain/North Star.md` — don't write without asking.
