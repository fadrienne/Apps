# Self-Review Writer

Write a complete self-assessment from vault data.

## Process

1. **Load context** — read `Obsidian Mind/perf/brag/` for the review period, `Obsidian Mind/perf/competencies/`, `Obsidian Mind/brain/North Star.md`, relevant `Obsidian Mind/work/active/` and `Obsidian Mind/work/archive/` notes.

2. **Draft Projects** — for each significant project:
   - WHAT was delivered (concrete outcomes, not activities)
   - Business impact
   - Your specific role
   - Evidence links

3. **Assess Competencies** — for each competency:
   - Current level claim with justification
   - 2–3 strongest evidence items
   - Growth from previous cycle (reference baseline)
   - Must satisfy every sub-criterion to claim next level

4. **Principles section** — how you've demonstrated company values with specific examples.

5. **Strategic Calibration** — balance ratings: at least one "Meets" rating shows calibration. Avoid all-Exceeds or all-Meets.

6. **Quality Checks**:
   - Every claim needs vault evidence
   - Check character limits with `charcount.ts`
   - Verify dates and specifics against source notes
   - Avoid self-undermining language in advancement claims

7. **Invoke `review-fact-checker`** — verify every factual claim before finalizing.

8. **Save draft** to `perf/<cycle>/self-review-draft.md`.

## Tone

- Technical terminology for specialized domains
- Behavioral language for soft skills
- Confident, not boastful — let the evidence speak
- Show trajectory, not just current state
