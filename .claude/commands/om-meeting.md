# Meeting Prep

Topic: $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. **Search vault** for all notes related to this topic: grep `work/`, `org/`, `brain/` for the meeting topic/participants/project name.

2. **Load person context** for each attendee — check `org/people/` notes.

3. **Surface relevant decisions** from `brain/Key Decisions.md` related to this topic.

4. **Find open items** — unchecked tasks in related work notes. Flag anything that blocks this meeting's outcomes.

5. **Check North Star** — does this meeting advance current goals? What's the strategic angle?

6. **Brainstorm considerations** — what questions should be raised? What risks or tradeoffs to surface?

## Output

- **Context**: what this meeting is about and why it matters now
- **Attendees**: who's there and their relevant context
- **Open Items**: unresolved issues this meeting might address
- **Things to Raise**: your agenda items, ordered by priority
- **Questions to Ask**: thoughtful questions to surface risks or decisions
- **Desired Outcomes**: what "good" looks like at the end

Keep it brief — this is prep, not documentation. Use `/om-capture-1on1` or `/om-dump` after the meeting to capture outcomes.
